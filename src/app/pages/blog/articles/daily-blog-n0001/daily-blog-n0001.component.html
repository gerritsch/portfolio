The Morning Routine

The short version of my morning routine is down below.

The first habit I would love to adapt again is to have a strong morning routine where I wake up early. Last year in january I had an extremly productive morning routine for several month until my environment started to change and I failed to adapt my morning routine to the new situation.

Last year, I woke up between 3:00 and 3:30 am and started working on personal projects or university related things, eating breakfast and meditate before hitting the gym at 8 am every single day. But my environment changed drastically since then and hitting the gym is currently not possible because of the corona pandemic. Therefore, I need to adapt a completly new routine which is more suitable for the long term since waking up between 3 and 3:30 am every single day was only possible for a few month.

For the next 30 days, I will try a new routine, which focuses on waking up early to exercise and meditate before I need to start working or attending lectures. Since a successfully morning always starts the night before I intend to also adapt a very short evening routine to make my mornings a lot easier.

At 18:30 I will start to write my daily blog post for an hour before getting ready for bed between 19:30 and 20:00. After being ready for bed I lay out my running clothes for the next day and set my alarm clock in the bedroom and turn off my phone and let it charge in the living room to prevent myself from laying in bed the next morning and scrolling endlessly trough the news or social media when I actually wanted to start exercise. After my environment is prepared for the next day I go to bed at around 20:30 in order to get my 7 to 8 hours of sleep. The next morning I wake up at 4:30 am and get out of bed and start to drink atleast 500ml of water and putting on my running clothes which I layed out the night before. After getting more awake I hope to hit the road at 5 am and start to run for an hour following a running plan from Adidas Running. After my morning exercise I shower and get ready for the day. My last two activities of the morning routine is to get a rich breakfast and meditate afterwards.

See you tomorrow.
